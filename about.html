About Me

Hello! I’m Camren Khoury, a third-year student at Clemson University majoring in Computer Engineering and Electrical Engineering. I have a deep passion for technology and the ways in which engineering can transform the world around us. My academic journey so far has equipped me with a solid foundation in both hardware and software, and I am eager to continue applying these skills to real-world challenges.

Academic Journey

Throughout my time at Clemson, I have focused on honing my skills in various technical areas, such as data structures, algorithms, electronics, and microcontroller systems. Some of the key projects I’ve worked on include:

  • Data Structures and Algorithms: Developing systems to manage and optimize data, including designing and debugging memory heap management systems, linked lists, and more.
  • Electronics and Circuit Design: Engaging in hands-on labs where I’ve worked with PIC32 microcontrollers, designed circuits, and developed solutions using sensors, comparators, and other components.
  • Embedded Systems: Creating systems for embedded applications, such as memory management systems for memory allocation policies and the implementation of simulators for complex systems.

I’m also continuously learning new programming languages and technologies. Currently, I am advancing my knowledge of Python, Swift, and C++, and I strive to gain proficiency in new tools and techniques that can expand my career prospects.

Career Goals

Looking ahead, my goal is to contribute to the field of embedded systems and microcontroller-based solutions. I am particularly interested in designing systems that bridge the gap between software and hardware, ensuring that both components interact seamlessly. My ambition is to work in a field where I can use my skills to design intelligent systems that solve real-world problems.

I aim to pursue opportunities that involve:

  • System Design and Optimization: Helping companies design efficient and innovative systems that can improve the performance of everyday devices.
  • Embedded Systems Development: Working on the development of microcontroller-based solutions for industries like healthcare, automation, and consumer electronics.
  • Research and Development: Engaging in the R&D of cutting-edge technology, particularly in areas such as AI, IoT, and robotics.

Why This Field?

The intersection of hardware and software has always fascinated me. Whether it’s through embedded systems, microcontrollers, or the complex algorithms behind various technologies, I love solving problems by combining both worlds. This passion for finding innovative solutions drives my academic and career ambitions.

Personal Interests

When I’m not immersed in engineering projects, you can find me on the basketball court, working on my physical health and improving my athletic performance, or swimming to maintain a healthy balance between my academic and personal life. Additionally, I enjoy problem-solving challenges, whether they are coding problems or logic puzzles.
